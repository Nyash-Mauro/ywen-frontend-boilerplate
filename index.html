About Us

YWEN is one of the very few grassroots organizations that works to ensure that AGYW from rural areas do have a voice within spaces that discuss issues that affect them. We wish to continue advocating for the full engagement of AGYW in all their diversity. YWEN wishes to fill gaps by coordinating young women and girls at risk and those that are living with HIV and AIDS have the right to be directly involved in all decisions that affect their lives.

Our Beginning

With the aim to end HIV related stigma and discrimination, one of the women living with HIV established Young Women Empowerment Network (YWEN), the organization got registered under the ministry of Health and Social services in 2016. YWEN works with AGYW infected by/with HIV and those that are at risk of contracting HIV. The population that is targeted is within the rural area in the 4 regions that the organization is operating from.

Our Mission

We spread our wings and influence.Where the interests of adolescent girls and young women, including those living with HIV and AIDS and at risk of contraction, are fulfilled and protected against stigma and discrimination.AGYW living in marginalized communities and disadvantaged backgrounds enjoy their full human rights free of poverty, violence or abuse, descrimination and social exclusion on the basis of gender.Where SRHR are not prioritized and not politicized.

Our Vision

improve living conditions of young women and girls in Namibia by their socio-economic empowerment in an environment free of stigma and discrimination.

Our Plans

YWEN will operate in all regions of Namibia and with a representative at regional level. YOUNG WOMEN EMPOWERMENT NETWORK will first start its operations in the regions of : Oshana, Oshikoto, Omusati and ohangwena. A Head office will be set in Ondangwa, Northern part of Namibia
